Prof. Dr. S.M.Balakrishnan
Principal’s Message

Government Law College, Madurai epitomises the lofty ideals of a healthy institution such as knowledge, research, human values, discipline and social audit, where freedom of thought and expression transcends infinity as we strive towards the Constitutional goal of achieving an egalitarian society through quality legal education and social engineering. This institution is powered by intellect and driven by values. Established in the year 1974 and affiliated to the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University, GLC Madurai is a pioneer institution catering to the needs of the southern districts of Tamil Nadu. It offers B.A.LL.B. Integrated degree program (five years), LL.B. degree program (three years) and LL.M. degree program (two years) in ‘Property Law’ and ‘International Law and Justice’. Over the years, our institution has produced eminent jurists, erudite scholars, impeccable judges, socially responsible lawyers, intelligent academicians, politicians and bureaucrats with integrity. This institution boasts of a robust legal education system with law teachers of high caliber who dedicate themselves to the welfare of the students and are highly motivated to prepare our students for trans-national practice. This college has a well-equipped library, e-zone and wonderful moot club, apart from a vibrant sports association, National Service Scheme and Youth Red Cross. A day in this institution begins with opportunity and ends with achievement.
